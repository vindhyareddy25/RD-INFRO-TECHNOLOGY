\# Customer Churn Prediction



\## Problem Statement 

Customer Churn is a major problem in subscription-based businesses. Predicting which customers are likely to churn helps businesses improve customer retention. Customer Churn affects business growth because losing customers leads to loss of revenue. This project aims to help companies identify customers who are likely to churn so that they can improve their retention strategies.



\## Objective

Build a machine learning model to predict whether a customer will churn based on their account and service details.



\## Business Goal

Reduce customer loss by identifying at-risk customers at an early stage.



\## Machine Learning Objective

Binary Classification



\## Input Features

\- Gender

\- SeniorCitizen

\- Partner

\- Dependents

\- Tenure

\- PhoneService

\- InternetService

\- Contract

\- PaymentMethod

\- MonthlyCharges

\- TotalCharges



\## Target Variable 

Churn(yes/No)
